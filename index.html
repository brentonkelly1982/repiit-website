Build. Play. Burn

Repiit is a powerful and intuitive workout app that transforms your fitness routine with intelligent voice guidance and flexible workout creation. Build custom workouts with both timed intervals and rep-based exercises, organize them into rounds and sets, and let Repiit guide you through every session with motivational voice coaching.

Create Your Perfect Workout: Design custom workouts from scratch or start with pre-configured routines. Add exercises with precise timing (like 45-second intervals) or rep counts (like 20 push-ups), set rest periods between exercises, and organize everything into rounds with multiple sets. Whether you're doing HIIT cardio, strength training, or mixed workouts, Repiit adapts to your training style.

Voice Guidance: Experience hands-free workouts with intelligent voice coaching that announces each exercise, provides countdown timers, offers motivational praise, and keeps you informed about what's coming next. Choose from multiple voice options and let Repiit be your personal trainer, keeping you focused and motivated throughout your entire session.

Flexible Workout Types: Support both timed exercises (perfect for HIIT, cardio, and circuit training) and rep-based exercises (ideal for strength training and bodyweight workouts). The app automatically detects workout types and provides appropriate timing displays and controls.

Intuitive Workout Management: Easily create, edit, and organize your workouts with a drag-and-drop interface. Reorder exercises and rounds, adjust timing and reps, and manage multiple workout routines. The app includes pre-configured workouts to get you started immediately.

Data Portability: Export your workout data as base64-encoded text to share with friends or transfer between devices. Import workouts from other users or backup your custom routines. Your fitness data stays with you.

Professional Features: Keep your screen awake during workouts, pause and resume sessions, skip rest periods when needed, and track your progress with visual progress indicators. The app includes comprehensive settings for voice preferences and data management.

Perfect for Any Fitness Level: Whether you're a beginner looking for guided workouts or an advanced athlete creating complex training programs, Repiit scales to your needs. The app includes sample workouts covering full-body routines, targeted muscle groups, and cardio sessions to help you get started.

Build. Play. Burn. - Repiit turns your workout vision into reality with intelligent guidance that keeps you motivated and on track to achieve your fitness goals.
