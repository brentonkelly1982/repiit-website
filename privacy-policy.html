Repiit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 28, 2025

Introduction

Repiit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Workout Data: We store your custom workouts, exercise routines, and fitness preferences locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • Settings Data: We store your app settings, including voice preferences and other customization options, locally on your device.

Device Information

  • Device Type: We may collect information about your device type and operating system to ensure optimal app performance.
  • App Usage: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app functionality and user experience.

Voice and Audio

  • Voice Settings: We store your selected voice preferences for workout guidance.
  • Speech Synthesis: The app uses device speech synthesis to provide voice guidance during workouts. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage

  • Workout Data: Your custom workouts, exercises, and routines are stored locally on your device for personal use.
  • Settings: Your app preferences and voice settings are stored locally to personalize your experience.

App Functionality

  • Voice Guidance: We use your voice preferences to provide personalized workout coaching.
  • Data Export/Import: We enable you to export and import your workout data using base64 encoding for data portability.

Analytics and Improvement

  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve app performance and user experience.
  • Crash Reports: We may collect crash reports to identify and fix technical issues.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your workout data and settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • We do not transmit your personal workout data to external servers.
  • Your data remains under your control and is not accessible to us.

Data Export/Import

  • When you export data, it is converted to base64 format for easy sharing and transfer.
  • Imported data is processed locally on your device.
  • We do not store or access your exported or imported data.

Security Measures

  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your locally stored data.
  • We do not collect or store sensitive personal information such as health records or biometric data.

Third-Party Services

Expo Services

  • Our app is built using Expo, which may collect anonymous usage analytics.
  • Expo's privacy policy applies to their services: https://expo.dev/privacy

Device Speech Synthesis

  • We use your device's built-in speech synthesis for voice guidance.
  • No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • You have full control over your workout data stored locally on your device.
  • You can delete all app data through the app's settings at any time.
  • You can export your data for backup or transfer to other devices.

Voice Settings

  • You can choose from available voice options on your device.
  • You can disable voice guidance entirely if preferred.

Data Deletion

  • You can delete all app data through the "Delete Data" function in settings.
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Your workout data and settings are retained locally on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your personal data on our servers.

International Users

If you are using our app from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information regarding the disclosure of your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. Since we do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes, no such disclosure occurs.

Your Consent

By using our app, you consent to the collection and use of information as outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our app.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of the Repiit app.
